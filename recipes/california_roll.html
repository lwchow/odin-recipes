California Roll

Ingredients

  • Cucumber
  • Crab sticks
  • Mayonnaise
  • Nori
  • Sushi rice
  • Sesame seeds
  • Avocado

Steps

  1. Slice the cucumber.
  2. Stir the crab sticks and mayonnaise.
  3. Cover nori with rice.
  4. Add the crab, avocado, and cucumber to the rice.
  5. Roll the nori and slice.
